Privacy Policy Of CelluCare

At CelluCare, accessible from https://cellucare-live.netlify.app, protecting the privacy of our visitors is one of our primary concerns. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to ensure your privacy while visiting our website.

If you have any questions or need further clarification regarding our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.

Log Files

Like many other websites, CelluCare follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they access our site. Hosting companies typically perform this step as part of their hosting services' analytics. The information collected in log files includes IP addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamps, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This data is not linked to any personally identifiable information and is used for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movements on the website, and gathering demographic data.

Cookies and Web Beacons

CelluCare uses cookies, which are small files placed on your device to help enhance your experience on our website. These cookies store information such as visitors' preferences and the pages they have accessed or visited on our website. We use this information to optimize your browsing experience and to personalize our web content based on your browser type and other factors.

Our Advertising Partners

Some of the advertisers on our website may use cookies and web beacons. These technologies allow third-party advertisers to serve relevant ads to users and collect data about users' interactions with the ads. The advertising partners listed below may use their own Privacy Policies to govern how they handle user data, and we have provided links to their Privacy Policies for your convenience.

Privacy Policies of Third-Party Advertisers

Please refer to the privacy policies of our advertising partners for more detailed information on their practices and how to manage your data preferences. Our advertising partners may use cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons in their advertisements and links that appear on CelluCare, which are sent directly to your browser. These technologies help them track the effectiveness of their ads and customize the ads you see while visiting websites.

Please note that CelluCare does not have access to or control over the cookies that third-party advertisers use.

CelluCare's Privacy Policy applies only to our website. It does not extend to other websites or third-party ad networks. You can manage cookie preferences directly through your browser settings. For more information on how to manage cookies in specific browsers, please visit the browser’s help or support page.
